# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We aim to provide a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Give actionable feedback
- Focus on ideas, not individuals

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive or intentionally harmful behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This applies across project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels.
